About Caching

Caching is a critical performance optimization technique that reduces database load and improves response times in modern applications.

Redis is widely used for caching due to its speed and flexible data structures, supporting patterns like cache-aside, write-through, and pub/sub messaging.

Understanding TTL, eviction policies, and hit rate optimization is essential for building efficient and scalable caching systems.
